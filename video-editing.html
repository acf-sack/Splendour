Video Editing

THEME

AI : Participants must explore the theme of AI in their submissions.

ELIGIBILITY AND PARTICIPATION

  • The competition will be held in two categories
    • Grade 6-9 Students
    • Grade 10-13 Students

GUIDELINES

  • Videos must prominently feature the theme of Ai. This can include but is not limited to:
    • Showcasing AI technologies or innovations.
    • Telling a story involving Ai or robotic characters.
    • Exploring ethical, social, or futuristic implications of AI.
    • Highlighting AI in industry, research, or daily life.
  • You can use any AI tool
  • Videos should be between 3 to 5 minutes in length, including credits.
  • Acceptable formats may include MP4, MOV, or other common video formats suitable for viewing and evaluation.
  • Minimum HD (1080p) resolution is recommended for clarity.
  • Ensure audio quality is clear and enhances the overall viewing experience.

JUDGING CRITERIA

  • Creativity and originality in storytelling and interpretation of the AI theme.
  • Technical proficiency in video editing, including editing techniques, transitions, and pacing.
  • Visual and audio quality, including clarity, composition, and use of effects.
  • Adherence to the theme and how well the video represents AI.
  • Overall impact and emotional resonance of the video.
