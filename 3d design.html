3D Design

THEME

Robotics: Participants must explore the theme of robotics in their submissions.

ELIGIBILITY AND PARTICIPATION

  • The competition will be held in two categories
    • Grade 6-9 Students
    • Grade 10-13 Students
  • Participants must participate individually.

GUIDELINES

  • The 3D design must reflect the theme of robotics.
  • Designs must be original and created specifically for this competition.
  • File Format: Acceptable formats may include STL, OBJ, FBX, or other common 3D formats suitable for viewing and evaluation.
  • Participants should submit a brief description of their design concept and any relevant details (e.g., inspiration, intended functionality).

JUDGING CRITERIA

  • Creativity and originality in interpreting the theme of robotics.
  • Technical skill in 3D modeling, including quality of modeling, texturing, and rendering.
  • Adherence to the theme and how well the design represents robotics.
  • Innovation and practicality of the design concept.
  • Presentation quality, including clear visuals and optional supplementary materials (e.g., concept sketches, renderings).
