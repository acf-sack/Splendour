Web Design

THEME

Robotics: Participants must explore the theme of robotics in their submissions.

ELIGIBILITY AND PARTICIPATION

  • The competition will be held in two categories
    • Grade 6-9 Students
    • Grade 10-13 Students
  • Participants must participate individually.

GUIDELINES

  • Participants are required to design a website that explores and showcases the theme of robotics.
  • The website should be designed to be responsive and accessible on multiple devices (desktop, tablet, mobile).
  • It must include original content, including text, images, and multimedia elements related to the theme of robotics.
  • Participants are encouraged to use modern web design techniques and frameworks, but the focus should be on creativity and thematic relevance rather than technical complexity.
  • The website should have clear navigation and user-friendly interface design.
  • Content must be accurate, engaging, and relevant to the theme.
  • Multimedia elements (images, videos, animations) should enhance the user experience and align with the robotics theme.
  • Content management systems (CMS) and platforms such as WordPress are prohibited.

JUDGING CRITERIA

  • Creativity and Innovation: How effectively the website integrates and explores the theme of robotics.
  • Visual Design: Quality of design elements, including layout, typography, color scheme, and use of imagery.
  • User Experience (UX): Ease of navigation, clarity of information, and overall usability.
  • Technical Implementation: Functionality of interactive elements, responsiveness across devices, and adherence to web standards.
  • Overall Impact: How compelling and memorable the website is in conveying the theme and engaging the audience.
