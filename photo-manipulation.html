Photo Manipulation

THEME

Robotics: Participants must explore the theme of robotics in their submissions.

ELIGIBILITY AND PARTICIPATION

  • The competition will be held in two categories
    • Grade 6-9 Students
    • Grade 10-13 Students
  • Participants must participate individually.

GUIDELINES

  • Participants are required to create a photo manipulation that prominently features robotics as the central theme.
  • Entries must be original creations and should not have been previously published or submitted to other competitions.
  • You are free to use any textures,overlays for your artwork.
  • Each participant may submit one entry.
  • Entries should be submitted in digital format (JPEG, PNG, etc.) with a resolution of [1280 x 720pixels].

JUDGING CRITERIA

  • Creativity and Interpretation of the Theme: How effectively the entry explores the theme of robotics through photo manipulation.
  • Technical Skill: Quality of photo editing techniques, including blending, lighting adjustments, color grading, and overall execution.
  • Artistic Merit: Composition, visual impact, storytelling ability, and aesthetic appeal.
  • Relevance to Theme: How well the entry aligns with the competition's theme and conveys a compelling narrative or message related to robotics.
