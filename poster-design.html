Poster Design

THEME

Robotics: Participants must explore the theme of robotics in their submissions.

ELIGIBILITY AND PARTICIPATION

  • The competition will be held in two categories
    • Grade 6-9 Students
    • Grade 10-13 Students
  • Participants must participate individually.

GUIDELINES

  • Participants are required to design a poster that creatively explores the theme of robotics.
  • Posters must be original creations and should not have been previously published or submitted to other competitions.
  • Dimensions should be [A3 size or 11"x17"].
  • Participants can use any digital or traditional medium (e.g., illustration, photography, digital art) to create their posters.
  • The poster should include the text elements such as title, theme description, and any necessary sponsor logos or competition branding.
  • Submissions must be in high-resolution digital format (PDF, JPEG, or PNG) suitable for printing.
  • The poster design should effectively communicate the theme of robotics through visual elements, typography, and composition.
  • It should be visually compelling, with a clear focal point and balanced use of color and imagery.
  • Text elements (if any) should be legible and complement the overall design without overwhelming it.

JUDGING CRITERIA

  • Creativity and Interpretation of the Theme: How effectively the poster design explores and interprets the theme of robotics.
  • Visual Impact: Overall aesthetic appeal, use of design elements (color, composition, typography), and ability to attract and hold viewer attention.
  • Clarity and Communication: How well the poster conveys its message or narrative related to robotics.
  • Relevance to Theme: How well the design aligns with the competition's theme and conveys a unique perspective or idea.
