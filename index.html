Competitions About Us

St. Anthony's College Kandy's Computer Fraternity hosts Splendour annually, celebrating ICT Day with a showcase of digital talent. This event features competitions in web design, photo manipulation, poster design, 3D rendering, logo design and game development providing a platform for students to demonstrate their skills and creativity in various tech domains.
